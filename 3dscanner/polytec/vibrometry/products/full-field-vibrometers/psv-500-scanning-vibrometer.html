PSV-500

Scanning Vibrometer

Full-field optical vibration measurement

Full-field optical vibration measurement

With the PSV-500 Scanning Vibrometer, you reliably and precisely measure vibrations in research and product development. The deflection shapes and Eigenmodes detected help you with everything from NVH, acoustic and structurally dynamic issues to ultrasonic analyses, FEM validation and non-destructive testing (NDT).

Highlights

Non-contact, full-field vibration measurement with FEM-like spatial resolution

Measure even on tiny structures due to smallest laser spot

Measure through water and other transparent media

Available as compact laptop version and rack-type workstation

Open interface for your applications

Expandable to 3D measurement system

Versatile accessories and software options

Scanning Vibrometers adopt to your measurement needs

Whatever question regarding vibration measurement drives you, there is a PSV Polytec Scanning Vibrometer to provide the answer – for frequencies from DC to 25 MHz, capturing vibrational velocities of 0.01 µm/s up to 30 m/s, studying samples from micro to macro. The precise helium-neon laser (HeNe) with its small laser measuring spot is suitable for measurements on extremely fine structures and even measures in and through water and other transparent media. For challenging measurements on technical surfaces, on dark, biological, moving or rotating test structures, the new PSV QTec Scanning Vibrometer is the ideal choice.

Choose between solid rack type or flexible and portable notebook version. Whenever your requirements grow, benefit from upgrades and options or expand it to a full 3D scanning vibration measurement system.

Optical Accessories

PSV-G-500 Geometry Scan Unit

Integrated laser distance sensor to measure the sample geometry.

PSV-A-440 Optical Derotator

For axial measurement of rotating objects. Locks onto the rotation and allows measurements as if stationary up to 24,000 rpm.

PSV-A-410 Close-up Unit

For close-up measurements, particularly on small parts. Includes a set of close-up lenses for the video camera. Special PSV-A-CL-xxx Micro Scan Lenses for small shiny parts available.

PSV-A-CL-xxx Micro Scan Lenses

Optimized beam path for measurements of very small parts or mirror-like surfaces. Requires PSV-A-410 Close-up Unit.

PSV-A-HNeBF Helium-Neon Block Filter

Notch filter for improved laser spot visibility when measuring very small parts or mirror-like surfaces.

PSV-A-RLight Ring Light

Fiber optic ring light for illumination of small test objects. Requires PSV-A-410 Close-up Unit.

PSV-A-526 Front Window

Protects the scanning mechanism against dust, wind and acoustic excitation at high dB levels.

A-MIR-xxxx Mirror Sets

Polytec's mirror sets contain different sizes of mirrors to assist in the set-up of your measurement.

Tripods, Test Stands, Positioning Stages

VIB-A-T02 Tripod with Pan/Tilt Head

PSV-A-T18 Vertical Test Stand

Motorized positioning of PSV scanning head for small part testing.

Miscellaneous

PSV-A-013 System Cabinet

Ergonomic mobile workstation with storage for all parts and accessories.

PSV-A-560 Coherence Optimizer

Laser stabilization improves overall signal-to-noise ratio.

External Scanner Control

Allows for an additional control of the scanning mirrors by external voltage signals.

VIB-A-HEAD Headphones

Headphone with noise limiter to listen to the vibrometer signal.

PSV-A-430 Acoustic Gate Unit

Activates the gate input if a noise exceeds a certain threshold. Suitable for event trigger applications such as brake squeal measurements.

Scalable Automatic Modal Hammer SAM

Excitation of sample for experimental modal tests with repeatable force level. Broadly configurable and avoids double hits reliably.

Scalable Automatic Modal Hammer SAM

A-RET-xxxx Retro-Reflective Supplies

Selection of sprays, tape, sheets and micro glass beads for improvement of signal to noise ratio when measuring uncooperative surfaces.
