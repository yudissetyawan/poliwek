Texturing

Texture improvement on raw materials

Powerful production technology for surface treatment operations very much depends on defined tribological properties, right down to its raw materials. Texturing is a must, particularly when wear properties, lubrication or the bearing load of product surfaces needs to be subsequently optimized. Information about micrometer and nanometer resolution is crucially important to characterize the texture and to improve the methods and thus ultimately the products such as combustion engines or transmission components.

Depending on your individual requirements, Polytec offers you a variety of instruments that you can use to characterize the structure of surfaces even during the raw materials manufacturing process.
