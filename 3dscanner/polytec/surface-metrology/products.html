Optical 3D surface profilers

Optical 3D surface profilers from Polytec are innovative, high-precision and non-contact tools for characterizing the surface topography in 3D. They are based on the principle of white-light interferometry, which is also known as coherent or vertical scanning interferometry or coherence radar.
