Optical 3D surface profiling across industries

TopMap surface metrology systems are precision tools for inspecting precision components and its surface structure. The non-contact method of these white-light interferometers allows for measurements with a resolution down to the subnanometer range. Polytec optical 3D surface metrology has become standard in the industrial quality control. Potential areas of application range from automotive and aviation sectors, to the semiconductor industry, electronics, biology and medicine. Which is yours?
